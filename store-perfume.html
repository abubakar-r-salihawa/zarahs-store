The Art of Oil Perfume

Zarah's Perfume specializes in pure, concentrated perfume oils. Unlike alcohol-based sprays that evaporate quickly, our oils blend with your body's natural heat to release a continuous, subtle fragrance trail (sillage) that lasts all day.

Every bottle is handpicked and formulated at maximum concentration. Our White Cream, Royal Oudh, and Apy collection are designed for local and national perfume lovers who demand long-lasting sophistication.
